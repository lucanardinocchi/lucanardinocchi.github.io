PromptAI Consulting

Founder · AI implementation for construction companies and SME builders

The experience at CENTINA became the foundation for PromptAI Consulting. The thesis is straightforward: most construction companies know they should be using AI, but adoption doesn't stick without structured implementation, hands-on training, and ongoing accountability.

PromptAI delivers three things: an AI readiness audit that identifies where AI will actually move the needle, a hands-on implementation program with role-specific training on real work, and a monthly retainer that keeps teams current as the tools evolve. The results speak for themselves: 50% to 400% productivity improvements on AI-assisted tasks, with no drop in work quality.

What makes PromptAI different is that it was built inside the construction industry, not outside it. I don't sell AI theory to builders. I do the work, I know the tools, and I train people on their actual jobs. Platform built, design partner live.
